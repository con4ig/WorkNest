# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Employees and administrators of the WorkNest platform. Employees manage their leave and profile, while administrators manage projects and teams. The context is a professional, internal corporate tool.

## Product Purpose
WorkNest is an internal human resource and project management tool designed to streamline leave approvals, employee management, and project tracking. It aims to provide a centralized "nest" for all workplace operations.

## Brand Personality
Professional, secure, efficient, and minimalist. It should feel like a premium "Expert Tool" (similar to Linear or Vercel). Emotional goals: confidence in data security, calm through clear information architecture, and delight through smooth interactions.

## Anti-references
Generic corporate software (SAP, old Oracle), cluttered dashboards, flashy animations that distract from work, and "SaaS-template" look with overused gradients and generic illustrations.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Flash**: Information hierarchy must prioritize readability.
- **Expert Precision**: Spacing, alignment, and typography must be perfect to convey trust.
- **Subtle Elegance**: Use high-quality neutrals with very precise emerald/teal accents.
- **Contextual Comfort**: Dark mode must feel easy on the eyes for long work sessions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. Support for reduced motion and high contrast. Readable fonts and clear focus states.
