# Design

## Theme
Dark-first professional interface. High-contrast neutrals with emerald accents.

## Colors
- **Background**: `bg-zinc-950`
- **Surface**: `bg-zinc-900`
- **Primary**: `emerald-500`
- **Secondary**: `teal-600`
- **Text-Primary**: `text-white`
- **Text-Secondary**: `text-zinc-400`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Sans-serif, bold, tight tracking.
- **Body**: Inter or system sans, 14-16px.

## Components
- **Cards**: Flat or 1px border (`border-white/5`), subtle hover states.
- **Buttons**: Sharp or small radius (4-8px), solid colors or subtle outlines.
- **Headers**: Large, centered or left-aligned with distinct accent borders.
